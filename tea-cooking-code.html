How to make tea

  • Let suppose we are going to make tea for two people one for you and one for your love, Just kidding guys.

  1. Take 200 or 250ml of water and boil for 2-3 minutes at low flame.
  2. Now add sugar 2 tablespoon as default, you can add or reduce as your taste.
  3. Take 2 or 3cm ginger and black pepper and grind them and add them, do this if you want ginger tea.
  4. Now add tea leaf (chaypatti) about 1 tablespoon at low flame.
  5. Now wait for 5-6 minutes, you can play a song in this interval.
  6. Now boil the tea at high flame 2-3 times, as tea flowing out of utensils get it as low flame and again take to high flame when it comes to previous position.
  7. As you guys know without love there is no taste in the tea so make the tea with love and serve it with love. 

  bye bye !
